about_me

A little about who I am and what drives me.

I'm a cybersecurity student at Southern Utah University, focused on offensive security and red team operations. I spend my time breaking into lab environments, studying attack techniques, and building tooling that helps me understand how defenders think.

When I'm not studying for certs or competing in CTFs, I'm tinkering in my Proxmox homelab — spinning up Windows Server domains, simulating real-world attack chains, and practising CRTA-style scenarios.

My long-term goal is to work as a professional penetration tester or red team operator, helping organisations find and fix their weaknesses before adversaries do.
