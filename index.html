about_me

A little about who I am and what drives me.

I'm a cybersecurity student at Southern Utah University, minoring in Computer Science, with hands-on experience across security fundamentals, IT operations, and web development. I'm currently working as an Admissions Processing Assistant, supporting the full admissions lifecycle and engaging with prospective graduate and online students.

I enjoy exploring different areas of cybersecurity — offensive security, defensive operations, and cloud fundamentals — while tinkering in my Proxmox homelab, practising CRTA-style attack chains, and competing in CTFs.

I'm also a co-author of a published research chapter on privacy-preserving quantum computing, and I've contributed to community outreach and STEM initiatives through my work with Southern Utah University's OutreachMAD programme.

My long-term goal is to work as a professional penetration tester or red team operator, helping organisations find and fix their weaknesses before adversaries do.
